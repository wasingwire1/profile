I am Asingwire Wilber, a Software Developer and Graphic Designer based in Kampala, Uganda. With over 2 years of hands-on experience, I combine technical expertise with creative design thinking to deliver solutions that are both functional and visually compelling.

My journey started with a deep curiosity about how things on screens work — that curiosity grew into a passion for building them. I love the moment a client sees their idea turn into a real, working product.

I believe technology should simplify lives, not complicate them. Whether writing clean, efficient code or crafting visuals that communicate with clarity, every decision I make is guided by the user's experience and the client's goals.

When I'm not coding or designing, I enjoy exploring new tech trends, contributing to open-source projects, and mentoring aspiring developers in my community.
